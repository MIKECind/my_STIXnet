Implementing Control Panel Items Control Panel items are DLLs or executable (.exe) files that let users configure the environment of Windows. They are typically accessed by clicking an icon in the Control Panel. This section describes Control Panel items and explains how to create and register them so that they appear appropriately in the Control Panel.
